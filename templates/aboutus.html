About CS50

CS50 - "Introduction to Computer Science" is a free online certificate course offered by Harvard University. Taught by Prof. David Malan, the course is spread across 10 weeks. Each week includes a video lecture and a (challenging!) coding exercise. At the end students are required to submit a Final Project they have to build themselves or in teams. The course is self-paced, which means its great for working professionals and students alike. I would highly recommend it to anyone who wants to learn computer programming. You don't need any pre-requisite knowledge about coding. You just need a LOT of tenacity!
