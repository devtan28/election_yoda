Frequently Asked Questions


On the dashboard, you will find a link "+Create a new election". Click this link. It will take you to a page where you can start building your election. Remember before your start building an election, you need to have atleast one voterlist uploaded to ElectionYoda. You can upload a voterlist using the "+Create a new voterlist" link on the dashboard.
To create a voter list click the "+ Create a new voterlist". This will take you to a page where you can upload a new voter list. Remember you can only upload a .CSV file with two columns - 1. Full Name and 2. Email Address. Make sure that the file has a header line with these two fields. There should be no other columns in the file.
You can email your voters with a link - www.electionyoda.com/voterregistration" where they can register themselves and then vote. Make sure you share the Election ID of your election.
On the dashboard, click the "See Details" link next to your election to open the latest results of the elections.
In the election home page at the bottom you will option to open reports of the latest voting patterns.
In the election home page and voterlist home page at the bottom, under Settings, you will find the option to Delete the election or the voterlist. NOTE: once you delete the election all the data will be deleted from the ElectionYoda database due to privacy and security reasons. So be cautious before deleting any data.
